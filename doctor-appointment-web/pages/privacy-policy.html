Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

At MedBook, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and medical history (when provided).
  • Health Information: Appointment details, symptoms, prescriptions, and doctor notes (with your consent).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to improve your experience and analyze site performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Book and manage your doctor appointments
  • Send appointment reminders via SMS/email
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) and secure servers to protect your data. Only authorized personnel can access your health information.

4. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal data. We only share it with:

  • Doctors and clinics you book with
  • Trusted service providers (payment gateways, SMS/email services)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Contact us at privacy@medbook.com

6. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you via email or a notice on our website.
